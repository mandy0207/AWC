Essence of AWC

The name "Akash Welfare Club" holds a profound significance for us. In February 2016, we experienced the heartbreaking loss of our beloved brother, the late Akash Chaudhary Although he departed from our lives too soon, he left behind a treasure trove of cherished memories that will last a lifetime. It is our heartfelt endeavor to create a lasting legacy in the name of Akash. Through our work and initiatives, we aim to uphold his memory and make a positive impact in the lives of others.

On 31st March 2020, during a conversation with my brothers, the impact of the Covid-19 pandemic was at its initial stage. We reflected upon the suffering of people due to the lockdown and the urgent need for help in our community. Despite our genuine desire to assist, we lacked sufficient resources at that time. It was during this discussion that we made a firm decision - we would establish an organization dedicated to the welfare of society in every possible way, driven by pure intentions.
